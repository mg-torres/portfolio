Education Coverage in Colombia

Data Visualization

The aim of this project is to show and discover insights about the quality of the education in Colombia throughout the years. This was done with three interactive charts. The first one compares the schoolyear repetition rate in two given regions of Colombia. The second one shows the edication coverage in each region of the country for a given year. The third one shows the years with higher and lower education coverage in each region.
